Welcome to the E17 Art Trail venue availability map

Whether you're an artist or run a space you'd like to participate in this year's Trail, you can use this tool to discover locations taking part or list a new one.

Finding a venue

On the main map page, hover over the dropdown to see the different categories. If you're looking for a location to show your artwork, click 'available' and browse the markers that appear by clicking on them. Once you've found one you'd like to exhibit at, get in touch with the venuw to try and book it. Once it's booked, let us know by finding the venue on the map again and clicking 'edit' and setting the category to 'booked'.

Listing a venue

If you manage a venue that's not been listed on the map yet, click 'Add location' in the navigation bar and fillin the details. We'll approve it and let you know when it's live.
